Guide to Divine Quotes

1. Start by choose the genre you are intrested in. (default genre: wisdom)
2. Now that you've set the genre you simply generate as many times till you find something you like.
3. If you found something you like you can favorite it by pressing the heart symbol, saving it in your favorites list. (Limit of 10 quotes!)
4. To access the the list you simply press the bars symbol where you will find obviously your quotes and and a clear and save button. (Side note: Pressing clear will remove all quotes!)
